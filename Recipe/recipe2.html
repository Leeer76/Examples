Ohmergerd Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • 8 strips of bacon
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 2 cups chopped onion
  • 2 cups chopped mushrooms
  • 3 tablespoons butter

Directions

  1. In a non-stick frying pan melt the 3 tablespoons of butter and place the onions, bacon, and mushrooms in to caramelize at medium heat. while waiting for those to caramelize, mix the eggs and milk together in a separate medium to large sized mixing bowl.
  2. Once the bacon is fully cooked and the onions are caramelized, pour the egg mixture into the frying pan and start scrambling.
  3. Once the eggs have been cooked through, add the mozzarella and cheddar and mix until gooey. Turn off the burner, plate and season the eggs to taste and dig in.
