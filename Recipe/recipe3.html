Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen pizza (dealers choice)
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese




Directions

  1. Cook the frozen pizza per the specification on the packaging.
  2. Once pizza is cooked per package instructions take out of the oven and sprinkle on the cheddar and parmesan cheese, let sit for a few minutes to let the cheese melt or place back in the oven for a moment to speed up the melting process.
  3. After the cheese is melted cut the pizza to desired number of slices and enjoy.
